About Me

Hello and thank you for visiting my website. My name is Santiago Lago, I'm a self-taught Front End Web Developer from Argentina, currently learning Server Side languages and Cyber Security, with the objective of becoming a Full Stack Web Developer.

Information Technology is something I've always been passionate about, specially the Internet, which I belive is one of the most powerful tools created in the history of Humanity. The Internet is accelerating the world and IT is taking over almost every single aspect of our life, creating an unlimited amount of potential and opportunities. In the fast paced world of tomorrow, learning won't be a defined phase anymore, it will be a lifestyle. This is why I belive in a self-taught aproach to learning. We must understand how to Search, Filter and Absorb all the information avaiable online in order to keep up and provide value to the dynamic world of the future.
